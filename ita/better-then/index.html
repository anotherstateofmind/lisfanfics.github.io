Better Then

by IsraelBlargh


Summary

Chloe has been dead for five months, and Max can no longer live with the choice she made. She's tried to go back, but her powers are gone. Or are they just dormant? Could she trigger them again with the right motivation? She can only think of one way to find out.

Drama/Thriller with time-traveling mind twisters and tons of Pricefield. Inspired by Koethe and his awesome music.
